About us

Roomway was made with the vision to create one unified social network for people to get to know each other in the capacity of flatmates, eventually helping them find that perfect person to share a house or room with. Our matching algorithm along with search based filters help you find people, whose interests and goals align with yours.

Diverse Locations

Shortlist flats, college hostels and co-lives based on amenities, price and most importantly, the kind of person staying there. You get to choose the best option for you.

Connect easily

Find people who - want to move into the same city as you, work in the same company as you, or just someone who has the same interests as you.

Let our algorithm help

Our proprietary matching algorithm lets you find your perfect flatmate based on a quick personality test.

Free forever

We don't believe in charging you to use our service. Unless, you're feeling generous of course :))

Safe and secure

Verified profiles only. No BS, no scams. Your information stays confidential and isn't shared with anyone.
